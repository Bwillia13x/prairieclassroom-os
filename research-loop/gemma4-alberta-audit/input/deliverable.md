# Deliverable

- Primary audience:
  - Founder/product builder preparing a defensible novelty and impact narrative
  - Secondary audience: educators, judges, funders, or Alberta education stakeholders
- Output format: policy brief
- Target length: 2500-4000 words
- Required sections:
  - Executive verdict
  - What is genuinely novel
  - What is not novel or is overstated
  - Alberta-specific relevance and constraints
  - What would make the platform credibly game-changing
  - Priority recommendations
- Tone constraints:
  - Non-partisan, evidence-first, direct
  - Explicitly separate established facts, mixed evidence, and inference
- Decision or recommendation needed:
  - Whether PrairieClassroom OS should be positioned as a differentiated Alberta classroom-complexity platform
  - Which claims should be emphasized, softened, or deferred until stronger evidence exists
