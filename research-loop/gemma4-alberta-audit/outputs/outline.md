# Outline

## Main Judgment

PrairieClassroom OS is most novel when framed as a local-first adult coordination and classroom-memory system for Alberta classroom complexity, not as a generic AI lesson-planning product and not as a generic "Gemma app."

## Question Tree

1. What exists in the codebase today?
   - Gemma 4 dual-tier routing
   - Multimodal worksheet extraction
   - Planning routes with thinking summaries
   - SQL-grounded classroom memory
   - Complexity forecast and substitute packet workflows
   - Safety framing and approval gates
   - Pending real-inference baseline
2. What is Gemma-specific about the implementation?
   - Strong fit: multimodality, local deployment, thinking-enabled planning tiers
   - Partial fit: structured JSON contracts
   - Weak fit today: function calling, specialized Gemma variants, audio
3. What is novel relative to teacher-AI competitors?
   - Commodity: lesson generation, differentiation, family communication, translation
   - Distinctive: complexity forecasting, complexity debt framing, substitute/EA handoff, local classroom memory loop
4. Why Alberta specifically?
   - Formal complexity-team allocations
   - Strong multilingual demand
   - Collaborative support model
   - Public-body privacy obligations
   - Uneven AI readiness in schools
5. What would make the "game-changing" claim credible?
   - Time savings and handoff-quality metrics
   - Fewer missed follow-ups and better documentation continuity
   - Small pilot evidence with teachers/EAs
   - Real Gemma 4 eval baseline and production governance artifacts

## Stakeholder Lens

- Teachers: reduce planning and documentation burden, not replace judgment.
- EAs: improve morning coordination and student-specific context handoff.
- School leaders: need privacy, auditability, supportability, and measurable benefit.
- Province: software can reduce coordination tax, not substitute for staffing.

## Rival Interpretations

- Skeptical view: this is another teacher-AI wrapper with Alberta language added on top.
- Strongest counter: the workflow center of gravity is adult coordination under classroom complexity, and the local-memory architecture is more distinctive than the content-generation surface.

## Conclusion Path

- Verdict: high policy fit, moderate product-surface novelty, strong architectural differentiation, incomplete proof.
- Recommendation: position as a high-potential Alberta classroom-operations platform, not yet a province-transforming solution.
